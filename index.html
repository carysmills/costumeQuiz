You should be a zombie

Zombie is a classic costume for a reason. It's simple to put together but captures Halloween perfectly. All you need is some old clothes to rip up, fake blood and maybe some face paint. Add extra props and messy hair as desired.

You should be a pizza rat

If you don't know what pizza rat is, your first task is to watch the viral video in question. This costume is a conversation starter, with the right mix of risqué and gross. Tip: make a fake pizza slice from paper and add to a rat costume.

You should be royalty

Couples and singles alike can't go wrong with royalty, whether you mimic a real or fictional monarch. Start with a crown, which you can find at any costume store. Make it creepy with blood or zombie elements if you'd like.

You should be a sexy nurse

Dress up by yourself or put together a team. Make it as risqué as you want. Plus, if you're into gore, feel free to add some blood. Go to a department store to pick up some scrubs to get started.

You should be a Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtle

A Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtle is the perfect costume for someone going solo, as a couple or in a group. Depending on your time and skill, try putting together your outfit yourself. Hint: start with green clothes and channel your inner turtle.

You should be a ghost!

You don't seem to have much time to spend on your costume. Grab a sheet as quickly as you can, drape it over your head and spend Halloween as a classic ghost.
